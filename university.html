ASTANA IT UNIVERSITY

History

Astana IT University is a place for smart and talented youth of the country, which was opened in September 2019 at the initiative of Elbasy Nursultan Nazarbayev.

University mission

Astana IT University is the leading center of competencies for digital transformation in Central Asia, the aim of which is to train highly qualified specialists in the field of digital economy based on interdisciplinary technologies and ensure digital transformation through training, research and successful innovation.

University vision

Astana IT University is a leading center of competencies for digital transformation in Central Asia.

Global goal

Training of highly qualified specialists in the field of digital economy based on interdisciplinary technologies.
